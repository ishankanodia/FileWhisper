FileWhisper for Websites

The AI search engine for your website.

One command on your server. Five minutes later, your visitors get an “Ask our docs” search box that answers questions from your own pages, manuals and PDFs, and cites exactly where each answer came from.

How it works

Install like a developer tool, not a platform.

No vector databases to run, no pipelines to build, no AI team required. FileWhisper indexes your documentation on your own server and serves search from there.

1

Run one command

SSH into your web server and paste the install command. That's the whole setup.

curl … | bash
2

Pick your content

A setup page opens in your browser. Point and click through your server's folders to choose the docs, manuals and PDFs to include.

3

It indexes everything

FileWhisper reads your pages and scanned files and builds a search index, right on your server. Your content never leaves it.

4

Paste one script tag

Copy the snippet it gives you into your site's HTML. The AI search box appears instantly.

<script>

On your server

$ curl -fsSL https://filewhisper.in/install.sh | bash

Then, in your site's HTML

> <script src="https://yoursite.com/filewhisper.js"></script>

That's it. Developers trust search boxes, and your visitors already know how to use one.
